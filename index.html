Gatsby Liqour

Gatsby Liqour was founded in 1922 in West Egg, New York by Jay Gatsby.

Originally intended to supply lavish private parties, following Mr. Gatsby's

untimely death, we expanded into the direct-to-consumer retail market. Over the

better part of a centruy we have developed an unsurpased reputation as Long Island's

trusted source of high-end spirits and other intoxicating beverages. Please examine our

listing of major brands and reach out if there are any hard-to-find beverages that you

would like for us to special order on your behalf.
